SOUTH DAKOTA’S OLDEST OPERATING BREWERY
South Dakota’s first brew-pub
For 25 years Firehouse Brewing Company has been crafting a wide variety of beer styles in the heart of downtown Rapid City.
From hearty stouts and unique seasonals, to crisp ciders and lagers - the rotating menu has something for every beer drinker. Proudly micro - we brew on an 8 barrel system
FREE GUIDED TOURS
Taste & Learn
Join us on a free guided tour and taste a few of our current wines and brews. Tours depart at the top of each hour from Firehouse Wine Cellars from noon - 8 PM. No reservations required, ages 21+
FAMILY-FRIENDLY RESTAURANT
Family-friendly Restaurant
Our Restaurant is open daily and offers a diverse selection of specialties including locally sourced buffalo burgers, Rancher's Pie, barbecue with made from scratch sauces and more.

Families love our kid's menu, home-made root beer and free firehats!
INTERNATIONALLY AWARDED WINERY
Working winery and tasting room
We craft a variety of wines - from oak-aged reds and crisp white wines to sweet dessert and fruit styles - our knowledgeable staff will help you find a wine to love.
