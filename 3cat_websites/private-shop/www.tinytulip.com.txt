Tiny Tulip is a family run monogramming & gift business established in 2004.  We have one of the most unique assortments of monogrammed gifts available today & work very hard to offer a superior quality monogramming.  We are the best at what we do because each item is personally monogrammed by our team here in Tallahassee, FL and is double checked by another member of our team before shipment to ensure that you are receiving an exceptional product. With our large selection of preppy personal gifts, you will be sure to find the perfect item for a gift for yourself or someone special.
What sets us apart from our competitors is our Customer Service.  Our customers are important to us, if you have a question about your order, or a question about a product that you would like to order, please feel free to call us at 888-708-8547.  We will answer the phone, or if we are helping another customer, or it is after hours, our customer service team will get back with you as soon as possible.
