Welcome to Fabric Mart
We are glad you stopped by! Fabric Mart has been in business for 40 years. Our website was developed in 1999 to meet the needs of our customers. In addition to our online fabric store we have a retail store that is located at 3911 Penn Avenue, Sinking Spring, PA. We pride ourselves on our customer service and the ability to offer such great fabrics at tremendous savings. 
We are still a relatively small family business but have grown to include 22 employees. Call us during normal business hours and you will always be greeted by a live person ready to help with whatever you need. Thank you for stopping in!
